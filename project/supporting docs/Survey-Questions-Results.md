# Little Tikes Brand Equity Survey: Questions & Results

## Overview
This document presents all survey questions from the Little Tikes Brand Equity Survey alongside their corresponding results. The survey collected responses from 501 parents and caregivers to explore how they perceive toy brands, what stands out to them, and what could be improved.
